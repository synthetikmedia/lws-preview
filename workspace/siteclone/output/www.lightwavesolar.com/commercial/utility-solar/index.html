Utility Solar

Utility Solar

LightWave has an outstanding reputation among power companies.

We provide turnkey solar EPC and O&M services for utilities and solar developers, specializing in solar installations up to 10MW.

Utility Companies Trust LightWave Solar

Utility companies have trusted LightWave Solar to help their customers access solar energy in their homes and businesses. Now, many of those same utility companies are relying on LightWave Solar to help them build their own solar power plants.

How Does Solar Help Utilities?

Solar energy is often the lowest cost option for utilities seeking to build new power plants. In addition, solar helps utilities meet customer demand for environmentally conscious energy sources. The end result? Ratepayers get cleaner and more affordable power.
